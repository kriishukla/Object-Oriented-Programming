Reticents

Through this app, we aim to contribute to this significant problem by helping people connect with another person in a safer environment. Our app initially aims to connect person A to any random person B or a group of people they see passing by without having to take all of the initiative, making the job easier. If person A wishes to talk to person B, they will get notified if they both are under the same radar. As the world revolves around connections, there are high stakes that person B will acknowledge person A’s wish to communicate, which can make all the difference in them opening up to you and possibly forming connections for life!

We are also considering creating a feature that helps like-minded individuals connect at that moment. Also, we want to add a part if person X wishes to play football at 11 at night but knows no one in his friend circle intends to do the same. Then most likely, person A would not go to play alone, and his idea would die immediately. Different colleges, Schools, Workplaces, and communities will register this app, but initially, we want to start trials from IIIT Delhi.
